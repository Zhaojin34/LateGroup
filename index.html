Descriptions

Project Name: Evol Panda

Our project will be a web app and its main idea is helping people to manage and plan their daily activity and long tern goals by increase their motivation. Every new user will adopt a panda as their partner. They can create several kinds of tasks, and every time they complete their tasks, they will receive experience and supplies. A panda will level up and evolve as user keep finishing their tasks, and users can further cultivate their partner with their supplies.


User Stories

1. As a user, I want to sign up and login with username and password, so that I can access my account.

2. As a user, I want to create tasks, so that I can do planning and manage my time.

3. As a user, I want a dashboard to list out all my tasks, so that I can check and edit my tasks.

4. As a user, I want a calendar, so that I can check my tasks more clearly.

5. As a user, I want a page showing information of my panda, so that I can play with my panda.


Demonstrable Outcomes

Project Motivation

There are several web and mobile applications assisting people in scheduling their events, however, most of the former, and latter are aimed at a strictly professional lifestyle. Our application will use a combination of event planning, and general day-to-day management. The panda is to provide positive reinforcement – with the goal to ultimately support users in developing and maintaining good habits, to create a good balance between professional and personal events.

The group knows how to code functions locally but have not built/run a web application prior to this. It is anticipated that the group will need to learn basic to moderate database/server principles.
